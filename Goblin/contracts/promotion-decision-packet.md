# Promotion Decision Packet

No candidate can be promoted without a complete decision packet.

## Required Components

- candidate scorecard
- truth alignment report
- strategy rationale card
- experiment accounting ledger reference
- strategy methodology audit reference
- search-bias narrative summary
- approval history
- deployment profile
- risk overlay
- statistical decision policy key citations
- deployment ladder state at decision time

## Enforcement Rules

- Promotion packets must cite explicit policy keys from `config/eval_gates.toml` and cannot rely on free-text judgment.
- Promotion approvals are blocked below `observed_demo` ladder state.
- Material deployment-fit deltas require an explicit new deployment bundle reference.
- Challenger candidates in portfolio lanes must carry a promotion packet before challenger progression.
